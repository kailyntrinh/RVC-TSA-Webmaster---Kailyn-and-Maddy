More About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of students at Ravenscroft School who are passionate about technology and giving back to our community. We believe that everyone should have access to technology and the opportunity to learn how to code, regardless of their background or experience level.

What We Do

We organize service opportunities for students to get involved in their community and give back through coding, teaching, and community outreach. We also provide resources and support for students who want to learn more about technology and coding.

Why We Do It

We believe that technology has the power to change the world, and we want to help make that change happen. By giving back to our community through technology, we hope to inspire others to do the same and create a more inclusive and equitable world for everyone.
